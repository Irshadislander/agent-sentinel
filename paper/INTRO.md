# Introduction

## Motivation
Production agents now call tools directly, making policy bypass, plugin misuse, and ambiguous failures operational risks rather than purely model-quality issues.

## Research Focus
This work studies runtime capability enforcement as a measurable systems property: deterministic decisions, explicit denial explanations, and complete audit traces under adversarial pressure.

## Contributions
- Deterministic policy resolution with stable reason-code semantics.
- Structured audit outputs for explainability and traceability.
- Reproducible ablation and benchmark pipeline for causal comparisons.

## Paper Roadmap
Formal semantics and guarantees are defined first, followed by threat assumptions, metric definitions, and empirical results on robustness and performance.
